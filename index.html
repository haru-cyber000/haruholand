About Me

Hi! I’m Haruho Yamaguchi, a Japanese freshman at Ritsumeikan University. I’m in the College of Information Science and Engineering, and I’m studying in the ISSE course. I joined a badminton circle called NEXUS, and I play regularly to stay active and meet people from different grades.
One of my favorite countries is Thailand. I actually lived there for three years, and I really loved it. The food, the atmosphere, and the people were all great. What I miss the most are the tropical fruits—especially mangoes. The ones I had in Thailand were so sweet and soft, it’s on a different level compared to the ones in Japan.
In my free time, I enjoy watching football. I’m a big fan of the Premier League, and my favorite team is Arsenal. I especially like Ødegaard, the captain. He’s not only really good and stylish, but also fights hard every game. Watching him always excites me and gives me motivation.
I’m looking forward to learning more in my major and also enjoying university life with friends and through my hobbies.
